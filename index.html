Our Product

A mobile application to provide budget-conscious consumers the opportunity to enjoy healthy diets.

Grocery Shopping

Our application generates grocery lists with both healthy and affordable food options based on parameters specified by the user. These parameters include the userâ€™s budget, meal frequency, household size, dietary preferences, allergies, and the duration of time their groceries are intended to last.

Cost Saving

The items on the grocery lists generated are chosen with a cost-saving algorithm to allow users to afford as many healthy ingredients as possible from their preferred grocery store without having to clip paper coupons.

Meal Planning

The grocery lists generated also contain ingredients to be used in preparing well-balanced and healthy meals for which we provide recipes.
